About GO-JEK


GO-JEK is Indonesia's largest startup with > 19M app downloads since launch in Jan 2015. It is also the fastest growing startup in the world outside of China, posting 900x MoM growth since launch.

GO-JEK has recently set up an R&D centre in Bangalore, India, and is looking to hire the best hackers in the country.

GO-JEK's engineering team is present in three cities now: Jakarta and Jogjakarta in Indonesia, and Bangalore in India.
